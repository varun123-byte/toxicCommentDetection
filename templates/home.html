Details

The main aim of this work is to bifurcate the comments in few classes basis on which people can decide whether to go watching this video or not. These notions must be prevented from spreading among the people especially young generation who are intact with social media. The idea is to divide the comments into categories such as toxic, severe toxic, insulting etc and deciding a tag for the video as toxic or non-toxic.
